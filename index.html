Hello! I am an employee of the indie gaming company: Riot Games. My job is to design the many characters you play on games such as Valorant and League of Legends! It is my goal to design agents and champions with a big pool of variety and inclusive of all identity groups. If you are familiar with the games, Valorant and League of Legends, you are able to notice the many different characters and what makes each of them unique.

Some of the many characters I have designed are: Jett, Sage, Astra, Raze, Sova, Phoenix from Valorant, and Caitlin, Samira, Akali, Seraphine, and Miss Fortune from League of Legends!

I also take in community requests and note what characters I would design next according to the requests. Based on past community requests, I have been able to develop several new characters, all with different backgrounds. Please feel free to submit ideas and take part in the development of new, upcoming characters!
